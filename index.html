Fintech Ecosystem · 2024–Present

Kafi X — Smart Investment Platform

Mobile AppWeb PlatformUser FlowsWireframesPrototypesDesign System

Problem

Retail investors in Vietnam needed a modern platform to trade stocks, derivatives, and bonds — but existing tools were either too complex for beginners or too limited for professional traders. A unified ecosystem serving both audiences was missing.

Approach

Mapped distinct user journeys for both investor profiles. Designed a modular system that adapts complexity to user expertise — simple views for new investors, powerful tools for professionals — while maintaining visual consistency across mobile (KOA) and web (Kafi X Pro).

Outcome

A unified investment ecosystem live across iOS, Android, and web. Established the design language and component library now used across all Kafi digital products — including Kafi Bond and Kafi Cash as embedded sub-products.

Digital Investment · Cross-Platform

Kafi Bond — Corporate Bond Trading

Mobile AppWeb (x.kafi.vn)Bond TradingInformation Architecture

Problem

Corporate bond subscriptions involve complex financial data, legal terminology, and multi-step processes that intimidate retail investors — creating high drop-off rates before transaction completion.

Approach

Redesigned the information architecture to progressively disclose complexity. Guided users step-by-step through subscription flows using clear visual language to surface key data (yield, term, risk) without overwhelming detail.

Outcome

A live product at x.kafi.vn enabling retail investors to confidently participate in corporate bond markets. Full design coverage from user flows through production UI, integrated into the Kafi X ecosystem.
